The Data-Heart

This panel listens to the raw data-pulse of the world and puts it on the map. It reads live events from three public, keyless sources and flashes each one where it HAPPENS: an earthquake at USGS's exact coordinates, an edit in the country of the Wikipedia's language. No API key, no sign-up, no backend secret - just defensively parsed reality, in real time, in its own geographic place.
